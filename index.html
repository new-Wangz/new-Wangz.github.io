Dear Sir or Madam / Friends / John / Professor Smith / Mr. Wang ,

P1 :

I hope this letter finds you well . ( 凑字数 )

I am _ _ _ who _ _ _ . ( 自我介绍 )

My name is Li Ming and I am a senior student in a certain Chinese university .

I am particularly delighted to recall the past days when we _ _ _ together . ( 寒暄 )

( On behalf of the Students’ Union , ) I am writing this letter for the purpose of _ _ _ . ( 目的 )

I am writing to take this opportunity to extend my heartfelt appreciation / welcome / congratulations to you ( for the generous help / assistance you offered / gave me ) .

It is my great honor / pleasure to _ _ _ . ( 目的 )

Words fail me when I wish to express my sincere gratitude to you . ( 感谢 )
My appreciation to you for your generous help is beyond words . ( 感谢 )
Without / But for / If it had not been for your help / assistance , my life would have been very different . ( 感谢 )

I am writing with sincerity to make an apologize for my carelessness . ( 道歉 )

On behalf of _ _ _ ,I would be very pleased to invite you to the forthcoming _ _ _ to be held on _ _ _ ( 地点 ) in May 4 , 2023 . We would be honored to have you there with us . ( 邀请 )

There are some details as follow . ( 凑字数 )

P3 :

Thank you very much for your time and consideration / assistance .

I apologize for any inconvenience caused . ( 道歉 )

Please accept my sincere apologies for any inconvenience I may caused . ( 道歉 )

I am looking forward to your reply .

Should you have any further questions , please let me know .

I hope you can enjoy your work and life .

I hope you can find these proposals useful .

I hope all you will take part and attend actively .

We sincerely hope that you could accept our invitation .

If you are interested , please do not hesitate to contact me at the email provided below : xxxxxx@163.com before May 24 , 2023 .

P2 :

For one thing / To begin with / Moreover / Besides / What’s more / In addition / Finally / Furthermore

It is my great pleasure to make several practical suggestions to you .

I would like to propose two possible solutions to this problem .

建议信

  • Will you please _ _ _ ?
  • If you are so kind as to _ _ _ , I would be greatly honored .
  • Would you mind _ _ _ ?
  • Is it possible for you to _ _ _ ?

介绍活动
The forthcoming _ _ _ will be held in on _ _ _ ( 地点 ) in May 4 , 2023 .
This activity / conference will start from a welcome address by _ _ _ at 9 : 00 a.m. , which will be followed by the main event where _ _ _ .Finally , there is going to be a closing ceremony in which _ _ _ .

好处、优点
拓展视野

  • broaden one’s horizons
  • enrich one’s life
  • expand one’s scope of knowledge
  • enlarge one’s experience

放松心情

  • relax one’s minds
  • release one’s pressure
  • restore one’s energy
  • have a temporary escape from the academic workload

广交朋友

  • widen one’s circle of friends

自己喜欢
I personally find it interesting / educational / enjoyable / fascinating / humorous

家人喜欢
It enjoys a widespread popularity / reputation among my family members / friends

政府、民众喜欢
It has received an official recognition from the government / authorities and enjoys a widespread popularity / reputation among citizens / students

借口
I have been suffering from a severe flu / a bad cold since the beginning of this week . I have been firmly advised by the doctor that it would be unwise to undertake any distant travel in the near future .

志愿者
students / volunteers should have cheerful personality / own relevant experiences / specialize in English listening and speaking / good communication skills / strong sense of responsibility / be aged under 25
